In 2+ sentences describe who you are and what you do.

Hi, I am Boris! I am 29 and I come from Ljubljana, Slovenia. I like martial arts, all kinds of sports that involve some kind of board, keeping a healthy lifestyle, and nurturing my foodie habits on regular basis. I'm a biochemist by training but never followed the scientific career path getting into entrepreneurship during the last year of my MSc studies and taking over a large chunk of our family run business in field of scientific equipment. Currently, I oversee all of our purchasing and logistical activities while helping others with anything else that for some reason lacks attention in our company.

Why are you taking this course?

Being self-employed and wearing many hats in our family business, I have realized that I kept stumbling on things that seemed so easy for other people. Managing a website, an online shop, creating landing pages to test ideas, and similar tasks had to be outsourced and had a »Great Unknown« feeling to them. Coming from scientific background, I have always had an itch for finding out how things work. So many times have I watched with some sort of jealousy all my coding-savvy friends creating something seemingly out of nothing just by what seemed to be proficiency in another language. Every time this fueled my curiosity. Last year I have at last decided to scratch this itch of mine and finally acquire this toolbox that will allow me to explore and build my ideas.

What previous experience do you have with programming?

I do not have much previous experience with programming. I have successfully completed the Fundamentals of Website Development course in spring 2014 semester at HES and I am half way through the JavaScript course on CodeCademy.

I am working on a PC.
